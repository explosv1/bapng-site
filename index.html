Welcome

Professional networking rooted in Barrington and the surrounding communities.

Barrington Area Professional Networking Group (BAPNG) is a volunteer-led community of professionals, small business owners, job seekers, and community leaders who live or work in the greater Barrington area. We meet regularly to share ideas, make connections, and support one another.

Whether you're growing a business, exploring a new role, or simply looking to plug into the local community, you'll find a friendly, low-pressure space to learn, network, and give back.

About

About Barrington Area Professional Networking Group

Barrington Area Professional Networking Group (BAPNG) was created to bring together professionals at all levels who share a common goal: building a stronger, more connected Barrington community.

Our members include small business owners, corporate leaders, entrepreneurs, job seekers, and career changers. It doesn’t matter if you’re currently working, actively looking, growing a business, exploring what’s next, or just “trying to figure things out”. You’ll find a welcoming, supportive group here.

BAPNG is intentionally low-pressure and people-first. We focus on listening, learning, and making introductions that lead to real connections, not just business card swaps.

What we value

  • Genuine connections – We prioritize real relationships over quick transactions.
  • Collaboration – We share ideas, referrals, and resources so everyone can succeed together.
  • Community impact – Strong, inclusive local connections make for a stronger Barrington area.

BAPNG is designed to be approachable, friendly, and action-oriented. A great fit for both seasoned networkers and those who are just getting started.

Events

Meetings & Events

BAPNG hosts regular networking meetups in the Barrington area, along with occasional special events such as guest speakers, panels, and collaborations with local organizations.

Recurring meetings

  • When: 2nd Friday of each month, 10:00–12:00 AM
  • Where: Barrington Library (505 N NW Hwy) and other local venues
  • Format: Open networking, introductions, and discussion.

New visitors are always welcome. We encourage you to arrive a few minutes early to settle in and say hello.
